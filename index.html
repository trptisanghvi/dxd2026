Turnstile Murmurations

Data through Design 2026 • Echo(logies)

March 21 – April 5, 2026 • Trpti Sanghvi

Turnstile Murmurations (2026) transforms the turnstile into a responsive light sculpture driven by MTA ridership.

The result is a luminous tracing of the NYC's circadian rhythm, mirroring our collective cycles of congestion and calm. The subway becomes a murmuration: data flowing like a flock through the city.

The work exists in dialogue with the rich history of public art within the New York transit system. Explore the works that define our subterranean landscape.
